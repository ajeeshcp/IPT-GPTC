About the college

Institute of Printing Technology and Govt.Polytechnic College was established in 1967 by the Govt.of Kerala under Technical Education Department, in a serene place named Kulappully at Palakkad District. The Campus is spread out in a green, lustrous 33 acres of land amidst forest to the northern side of Shoranur Railway Junction. It has the privilege of the only Institute in the entire state which offers a three Year Diploma programme in Printing Technology. In addition the college also offers Diploma programme in Electronics Engineering and Computer Engineering. The college Campus possess state of the art facilities like an administrative block, Laboratory wing, Auditorium, Non conventional energy park, library etc.
Awards: Our former student Dr.Rajendrakumar Anayat who stood in 1st rank from the Institution in 1990 received Printing Industry Research Association [PIRA] academic excellence award.
